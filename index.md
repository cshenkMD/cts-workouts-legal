# Emilan — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 2026-07-03**

Emilan (formerly CTS Workouts) is built around a simple principle:
your workout data belongs to you, and stays with you. This policy
describes what information the app handles and how.

## What we collect

**Nothing.** The app does not have its own servers and does not
transmit your data to any third party. There is no analytics, no
tracking, no advertising, no telemetry.

## Where your data lives

All workout data you create — routines, schedules, sessions, set logs,
walks you record, notes, photos you attach to exercises — is stored
locally on your device.

If you are signed into iCloud on your device and have iCloud Drive
enabled, the app uses Apple's CloudKit service to keep your data in
sync across your own Apple devices. This data is stored in your
**private** iCloud database, accessible only to you under your Apple
ID. We do not have access to it. Apple's iCloud privacy terms apply.

## Permissions the app requests

All permissions are optional — the app works without them, with the
related features disabled.

- **Location (while using the app):** used only while you are
  recording a walk, to map your route and measure distance and pace.
  The route is saved with the walk on your device and in Apple Health;
  it is never sent to us or any third party.
- **Motion & Fitness:** used to count steps and detect walking versus
  standing still during a recorded walk.
- **Apple Health:** the app reads data you authorize — such as heart
  rate, walking distance, steps, active energy, cycling workouts, and
  body weight — to show your stats consistently with the Health app.
  It writes the walks you record into Health so they appear in Fitness
  and count toward your rings. Health data stays under Apple Health's
  control on your device and in your iCloud; we never receive it.
- **Notifications:** used to remind you when a scheduled workout is
  due. Notifications stay on your device.
- **Photo library:** used only if you attach a photo to an exercise in
  your library. Photos remain on your device and in your iCloud if
  iCloud Photos is enabled.
- **iCloud:** used for cross-device sync via CloudKit as described
  above.

## Apple services

Two features talk to Apple services (never to us or anyone else):

- **Weather:** the app fetches conditions for a walk from Apple's
  WeatherKit, which involves sending the walk's approximate location
  to Apple.
- **Place names:** the app turns a walk's coordinates into a readable
  place name (like "Bethesda, MD") using Apple's Maps services.

Both are governed by Apple's privacy terms.

## Third-party services

The app uses no third-party services. The exercise library bundled
with the app contains content from
[free-exercise-db](https://github.com/yuhonas/free-exercise-db), used
under its public-domain-equivalent terms.

## Children

The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly
collect any data from anyone.
